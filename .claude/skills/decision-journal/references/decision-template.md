---

## Good decisions vs. bad decisions in the journal

**Good entry:** Includes the "why", names at least one rejected alternative, sets a review condition.

**Bad entry:** "We decided to go with X." (Why? What else was considered? How will we know if it worked?)

The test: could someone 12 months from now understand *why* this was the right call at the time, even if they'd choose differently now?
