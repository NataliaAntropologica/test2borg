> The heartbeat auto-detects objective pillars and nudges on unchecked ones late in the day.
> Self-report pillars require Natalia to check them in Obsidian or chat.
> Heartbeat archives yesterday's checklist to History each morning and creates a fresh one.

## How pillars work

- **Morning reset:** Heartbeat archives yesterday's checklist and creates a fresh one each day
- **Auto-detection:** Objective pillars are checked by the heartbeat if evidence is found
- **Late-day nudge:** If unchecked pillars remain after 5 PM COT, heartbeat sends a reminder
- **Self-report:** For pillars the heartbeat can't auto-detect, check manually in Obsidian

## Customizing pillars

Edit the three pillars above to match what matters most to you.
For each pillar, specify:
- A clear, one-sentence description of what "done" looks like
- Whether it's auto-detectable (and what signal to look for)
